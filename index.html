list of books

The Dollhouse Murders
The Dollhouse Murders is a perfect pick for horror loving people.

It is a must-read and not only does it appeal children but adults and teenagers as well.A work of literature that is much loved around the globe. The plot is exceptional to say the least and I am sure that this is a book that deserves it's place on your shelf.

Coraline
Coraline is a mystery-horror-adventure novel by Neil Gaman The only word I can use to describe it is "OUTSTANDING".

It is a novel anyone can fall into. No matter the age or gender. The writing makes you feel like you're in the story. It teaches you to be content with what you have and appreciate it. It takes you through a bitter-sweet journey of fun, terror, love and victory. One of the best fantasy books ever written.Never judge a book by its cover because Coraline is just one of those masterpieces who get judged for their outer covers.Recommend.
